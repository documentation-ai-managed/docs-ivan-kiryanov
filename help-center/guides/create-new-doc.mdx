## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Docs section">
    ````tip
    Click "Documents" in the left sidebar.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'New Document'">
    ````tip
    Fill in the title and content for your doc.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    ````tip
    Hit "Publish" to make your doc live. That's it!
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
