## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have the following:

<Callout kind="info">

- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- An email address for account verification
- Optional: A GitHub account for version control integration

</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Sign up in just a few steps to access your documentation space.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="monitor">
    Open your browser and navigate to `https://dashboard.example.com/signup`.

    Fill in your email, create a strong password, and complete the CAPTCHA.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Your Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from Ivan Kiryanov.

    Click the verification link to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Onboarding" icon="check-circle">
    Log in and follow the short onboarding tour.

    Select your workspace name, such as `My Project Docs`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Set Up Your Initial Workspace

Customize your workspace to match your project needs.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Basic Setup" icon="settings">
    In the dashboard, go to **Workspace Settings**.

    Set your brand color to `#3B82F6` and add a description: "Welcome to Ivan Kiryanov's documentation."
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Integrations" icon="plug">
    Connect GitHub under **Integrations**.

    Authorize the app to sync your repositories automatically.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Enable version history during setup for automatic backups.
</Callout>

## Create Your First Document

Build your first MDX page right away.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Document" icon="file-plus">
    Click **New Document** in the sidebar.

    Name it `introduction.mdx`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Frontmatter and Content" icon="edit-3">
    Paste this starter template:

    <CodeGroup tabs="MDX">
      ```mdx
      ---
      title: Introduction
      description: Welcome to your docs.
      ---

      ## Overview

      Start writing here.
      ```
    </CodeGroup>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview and Publish" icon="eye">
    Switch to **Preview** mode to see live rendering.

    Click **Publish** to make it live at `/introduction`.
  </Step>
</Steps>
